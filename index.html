Attention Is the Only Moat in Memecoins

Been thinking about this a lot — the memecoin market doesn't reward fundamentals, it rewards attention capture. The tokens that survive aren't the ones with the best tokenomics or the cleanest contracts. They're the ones that hijack a narrative at exactly the right moment.

This is why I keep coming back to the attention arbitrage framework. You're not trading tokens, you're trading mindshare. The chart is just a lagging indicator of how many people care right now.

Mar 8, 2026
SOL Memecoins vs ETH Memecoins — Different Games

SOL memes move faster, die faster. ETH memes move slower but have stickier communities. Base is somewhere in between — cheaper gas than ETH, more institutional-adjacent vibes.

Your strategy has to adapt to the chain. What works as a snipe on Solana doesn't translate to Ethereum. Different liquidity profiles, different holder behavior, different exit windows.
